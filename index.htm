What's New

  • E-Learning

    The school understands the significance of electronic learning and has integrated technology in the curriculum in such a way that technology supplements the primary learning methods. DPS, Gandhinagar has five modern Computer Laboratories that can accommodate 180 learners at one go. The laboratories are equipped with Multimedia Projectors and hi-speed internet connectivity. All the computer machines installed have the latest application software, namely Windows 7 & Windows 10 Operating Systems, Open-Office, MS-Office, Adobe Photoshop, Flash, Dreamweaver, MySQL along with programming language C, C++and others. The computers are protected through firewalls that also restrict access to undesirable websites and have licensed antivirus software to keep viruses and malware at a distance.

  • Laboratories

    The theoretical concepts, formulae, and theories play a significant role in adding to the existing knowledge base of the students, but the inquisitiveness of the students gets better satisfied through practical experiments. The students study different concepts in various subjects, especially branches of Science. In Physics, they learn about projectile, in Chemistry, they get familiarized with litmus tests, and so on. When they test it themselves in the laboratory, they not only discover the authenticity of the concept, but understand its practical relevance and significance as well. This hands-on exposure pushes them to understand the practical nuts and bolts in a better way and prepares them better for research from an early stage itself.
